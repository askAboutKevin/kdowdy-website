Kevin is a class of '20 graduate of John Jay College of Criminal Justice where he studied Computer Science and Information Security with a minor in Security Management. As part of his studies he completed courses in Linear Algebra, Calculus, Computer Architecture and more. At EY he is a consultant in the Technology Advisory Program in their Engineering and Programming domain where he supports projects for clients in consumer industries. Kevin Outside of his career Kevin enjoys reading sci-fi and fantasy novels, discovering amazing food and spending time with his family and friends.
